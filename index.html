Built for Speed, Privacy, and Independence

No databases on external servers, no cloud subscriptions, and no tracking. Just professional tools.

100% Local & Offline

All client records, finances, and invoices are saved securely on your device's local sandbox storage. Complete privacy and GDPR compliance out of the box.

No Client & PDF Limits

Serverless Cloud Sync
