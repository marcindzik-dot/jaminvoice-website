Built for Speed, Privacy, and Independence

No databases on external servers, no cloud subscriptions, and no tracking. Just professional tools.

100% Local & Offline

All client records, finances, and invoices are saved securely on your device's local sandbox storage. Complete privacy and GDPR compliance out of the box.

No Client & PDF Limits

Create and manage unlimited clients, generate infinite PDF invoices and estimates. No paywalls, subscription models, or unexpected usage fees.

Serverless Cloud Sync

Sync PC, phone, and tablet without database servers. Just set a shared sync folder (e.g. Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox) and let JamInvoice auto-merge changes safely.

Professional PDF Engine

Generate premium, high-quality PDFs for print or email in seconds. Supports tax calculations, custom company logos, signatures, and localized currency options.
